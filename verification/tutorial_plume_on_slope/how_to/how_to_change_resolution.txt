Spatial resolution:

  At COMPILE time:
  
  change sNx, Nr in code/SIZE.h
  
  At RUNTIME:
  
  run gendata.py to produce input/ files with appropriate Nx, Nz values (make sure Nx = sNx*nSx*nPx etc)
  and change delZ in input/data/&PARAM04

Temporal resolution:

  At RUNTIME:
  
  change nTimeSteps in input/data/&PARAM03
  change deltaT in input/data/&PARAM03 to abide by CFL
